## Reading

- **Box mAP50 0.814 is the new high.** 0.036 away from the TZ target 0.85.
  Larger backbone (yolo11s, 9.7M params vs n's 2.7M) helps box localisation.
- **Pose mAP50 0.842 is also the best so far.** Above the OKS-0.5 bar with
  big margin.
- **Per-keypoint precision trades.** n_aug wins on `c` (5.3 vs 7.0 px),
  s_aug wins on `b` (6.4 vs 8.4 px). Pick depending on which keypoint
  matters most downstream — for AR raycast on the disc, n_aug is better.
- **FN / FP unchanged** between n_aug and s_aug — neither model finds
  more or fewer wheels, they just place keypoints differently.

The combined training (`wheel_real_v1_combined_s`, fine-tuned from this
checkpoint on 255 wheels) is the next step toward the 0.85 target.
