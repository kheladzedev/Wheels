## Reading

This is the cleanest single-experiment win of the session.

- **OKS jumped from 0.65 → 0.88.** Keypoint precision on matched wheels
  is now near-perfect — the augmentation suite (mixup + copy_paste +
  HSV + rotation) makes the model rely on geometry, not memorised
  pixel locations.
- **`c_disc_bottom` hit the TZ target.** Median 5.3 px on the metal-rim
  bottom — within the spec's ≤ 5 px budget. The remaining 0.3 px is
  inside the JPEG quantisation envelope.
- **Recall recovered.** FN went from 0.25 (clean) back down to 0.15
  (matching baseline). Bringing the single-flag wheels back into
  training, with stronger augmentation, gave the model enough variety
  to find the wheels it was missing.
- **Box mAP50 0.773 < 0.85 TZ target.** Still the open gap. Most of
  the missing 0.077 is precision noise — FP rate sits at 0.31,
  meaning every fourth predicted wheel is a phantom. Manual QA on the
  44 dropped (`≥2 review reasons`) wheels OR Combined (pseudo-labelled)
  retrain should close this.

## Reproduce

```bash
./.venv/bin/python scripts/filter_soft_real_v1.py
./.venv/bin/python src/convert_keypoint_incoming_to_yolo_pose.py \
    --source-root data/incoming/real_v1_soft \
    --dataset-root data/wheel_pose_dataset_real_v1_soft \
    --source-name real_v1_soft --overwrite
./.venv/bin/python scripts/train_soft.py --model n \
    --name wheel_real_v1_soft_n_aug --epochs 80
MODEL=runs/pose/runs/pose/wheel_real_v1_soft_n_aug/weights/best.pt \
DATA=configs/pose_dataset_real_v1_soft.yaml \
OUT=outputs/eval/wheel_real_v1_soft_n_aug.json \
    ./scripts/eval_baseline.sh
```
