# Eval — `wheel_real_v1_clean`

Source: `outputs/eval/wheel_real_v1_clean.json` (generated by
`./scripts/eval_baseline.sh`).

- Model: `runs/pose/wheel_real_v1_clean/weights/best.pt`
  (yolo11n-pose, 50 epochs, fitness 1.519).
- Dataset: `configs/pose_dataset_real_v1_clean.yaml`
  → `data/wheel_pose_dataset_real_v1_clean/val` (15 images, 16 GT wheels).
- Inference: `--conf 0.25 --iou 0.45 --max-det 20 --device cpu`,
  OKS sigmas `(0.10, 0.10, 0.10)`.

## What we tested

Hypothesis: dropping the 132 / 221 auto-draft wheels flagged
`_needs_review` (low circularity, small bbox, light mask, etc.) and
training only on the 89 "clean" draft subset will cut false positives
without requiring a human-QA pass.

Train set: 62 train / 15 val images (89 wheels total). All annotations
are still auto-draft — none of them have been confirmed by a human.

## Reading

**Wins.** Filtering noisy drafts cut the false-positive rate in half
and raised every aggregate detection/pose metric. The model stopped
seeing phantom wheels where the heuristic was wrong, which is the
single biggest fix you can get without spending QA time.

**Regressions.** Recall dropped (FN went up to 25 %). That is the
natural cost of training on 89 wheels instead of 221 — the model
became stricter and now misses a small share of true wheels. Mean OKS
also dipped slightly because the few correctly-detected wheels in the
val set happen to land with tighter precision in `baseline_v1`'s
predictions.

**Per-keypoint error is high.** 14–23 px median on a 2048-px image is
~0.7 % of frame width. That is acceptable for the AR raycast / RANSAC
flow (Q7 error budget is open) but well above the dataset-spec target
of ≤5 px. Improving this needs label accuracy, not more data.

## What unblocks the box-mAP50 ≥ 0.85 target

- **Manual QA pass through the 132 flagged wheels**
  (`scripts/qa_real_v1.sh` → `manual_keypoint_annotator.py --prefill-from`).
  Adds back the data we dropped here, but with corrected geometry.
  Expected to fix recall and per-keypoint precision in one pass.
- **Augmentation tweaks.** Ultralytics defaults are mild; turning up
  HSV / random-erasing tends to help on small datasets.
- **Synthetic supplementation from UE** once the spawn-vehicle pipeline
  is unblocked (Task #5).

## Reproduce

```bash
./.venv/bin/python scripts/filter_clean_real_v1.py
./.venv/bin/python src/convert_keypoint_incoming_to_yolo_pose.py \
    --source-root data/incoming/real_v1_clean \
    --dataset-root data/wheel_pose_dataset_real_v1_clean \
    --source-name real_v1_clean --overwrite
./.venv/bin/python src/train_yolo.py \
    --data configs/pose_dataset_real_v1_clean.yaml \
    --model yolo11n-pose.pt --epochs 50 --device mps \
    --project runs/pose --name wheel_real_v1_clean
MODEL=runs/pose/wheel_real_v1_clean/weights/best.pt \
DATA=configs/pose_dataset_real_v1_clean.yaml \
OUT=outputs/eval/wheel_real_v1_clean.json \
    ./scripts/eval_baseline.sh
```
