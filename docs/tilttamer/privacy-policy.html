TiltTamer Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TiltTamer handles information. TiltTamer is a local-first household tower stability planning app that helps users create item stacks, view center-of-mass feedback, estimate tilt risk, and share local safety summaries.

Summary

TiltTamer does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal information. The app does not require an account, does not use cloud sync, does not include advertising, and does not use analytics or tracking.

Your tower layouts, item presets, custom blocks, guide advice, unit preferences, summaries, and settings stay on your device unless you choose to share a summary using iOS system features.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users of TiltTamer through the app.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, payment information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, device identifiers for tracking, or app activity for tracking.

Local App Data

TiltTamer lets you create and store content locally on your device, including household item presets, custom blocks, dimensions, weights, colors, icons, center offsets, tower layouts, duplicated layouts, custom safety guide advice, unit settings, and exported summary content.

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not receive, access, or upload this information.

Sharing

TiltTamer may let you export or share a local layout summary using the native iOS share sheet. Sharing is controlled by you and by the apps or services you choose to use.

We do not receive or store anything you share through iOS system features.

Support Messages

If you contact support by email or Telegram, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your message, contact handle, device details, screenshots, or troubleshooting notes. We use support messages only to respond to your request, improve the app, and understand reported issues.

Third-Party Services

TiltTamer does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, backend servers, or cloud storage services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information. The app itself does not collect personal information.

Data Deletion

Because TiltTamer stores app content locally on your device, you can delete your data by deleting individual tower layouts or presets in the app, using any available local data deletion option, or deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

TiltTamer does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user through the app, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Safety Disclaimer

TiltTamer is an educational planning aid and is not a structural engineering guarantee. Real-world safety depends on factors the app cannot fully model. Always follow manufacturer instructions, avoid unsafe stacking, anchor tall furniture where appropriate, and consult a qualified professional for safety-critical decisions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
