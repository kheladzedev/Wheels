TiltTamer Support

TiltTamer is a local-first household tower stability planning app. It helps you build visual stacks, compare center of mass, support margin, total height, total weight, tilt risk, and safer ordering ideas before building in the real world.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does TiltTamer require an account?

No. TiltTamer does not require an account or sign-in.

Does TiltTamer work offline?

Yes. TiltTamer is designed to work offline. Your layouts, item presets, custom blocks, guide advice, summaries, unit preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Does TiltTamer collect personal data?

No. TiltTamer does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information through the app.

Where are my tower layouts stored?

Your tower layouts and custom items are stored locally on your device. TiltTamer does not use cloud sync or developer-operated backend servers.

Can I share a layout summary?

Yes. If you choose to export or share a summary, TiltTamer uses the native iOS share sheet. You control what you share and which app or service receives it.

Can TiltTamer guarantee that a real stack is safe?

No. TiltTamer is an educational planning aid, not a structural engineering guarantee. Real-world safety depends on many factors the app cannot fully model, including surface friction, material strength, anchoring, vibration, children, pets, impact, moisture, and product defects.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete layouts, presets, or custom items inside the app where available, or delete the app from your device to remove local app data.

Privacy

TiltTamer is built with privacy in mind. The app does not require sign-in, does not use ads, does not track users, and does not collect personal information through the app.
