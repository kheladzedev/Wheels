Frequently Asked Questions

Does Stackwich Forge require an account?

No. Stackwich Forge does not require an account or sign-in.

Does Stackwich Forge work offline?

Yes. Stackwich Forge is designed to work offline. Your sandwich towers, pantry ingredients, experiment plans, taste tags, preferences, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Does Stackwich Forge collect personal data?

No. Stackwich Forge does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share personal information through the app.

Where are my sandwich towers and pantry items stored?

Your towers, layers, sauces, pantry ingredients, availability status, experiment plans, and custom tags are stored locally on your device. Stackwich Forge does not use cloud sync or developer-operated backend servers.

Can I share or export my data?

If the app offers sharing or export, it uses iOS system features. You choose what to share and which app or service receives it.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual towers, ingredients, plans, or tags, use the delete-all-local-data option in Settings where available, or delete the app from your device to remove local app data.

Can I reset onboarding?

Yes. If available in Settings, you can reset onboarding without creating an account or contacting support.

Privacy

Stackwich Forge is built with privacy in mind. The app does not require sign-in, does not use ads, does not track users, and does not collect personal sandwich journal content through the app.

For more details, please read the Stackwich Forge Privacy Policy.
