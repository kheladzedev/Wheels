Stackwich Forge Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stackwich Forge handles information. Stackwich Forge is an offline-first sandwich tower journal that helps users create sandwich tower cards, manage a pantry, choose flavors by mood, schedule experiment plans, and keep local notes and preferences.

Summary

Stackwich Forge does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal sandwich records. The app does not require an account, does not use cloud sync, does not include advertising, and does not use analytics or tracking.

Your towers, ingredients, pantry availability, experiment plans, taste tags, preferences, onboarding state, haptic settings, appearance settings, and app settings stay on your device unless you choose to share or export something using iOS system features.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users of Stackwich Forge through the app.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, payment information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, device identifiers for tracking, app activity for tracking, or sandwich journal content.

Local App Data

Stackwich Forge lets you create and store content locally on your device, including sandwich tower names, bread types, layers, sauces, taste tags, mood tags, prep time, difficulty, satiety, notes, ingredient categories, flavor profiles, availability status, experiment plans, priorities, linked towers, custom tags, appearance settings, haptic preferences, onboarding status, and local app settings.

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not receive, access, or upload this information.

Sharing and Export

Stackwich Forge may let you share or export app content using iOS system features. Sharing is controlled by you and by the apps or services you choose to use.

We do not receive or store anything you share through iOS system features.

Support Messages

If you contact support by email, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your message, email address, device details, screenshots, or troubleshooting notes. We use support messages only to respond to your request, improve the app, and understand reported issues.

Third-Party Services

Stackwich Forge does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, backend servers, or cloud storage services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information. The app itself does not collect personal information or personal sandwich journal content.

Data Deletion

Because Stackwich Forge stores app content locally on your device, you can delete your data by deleting individual towers, ingredients, plans, or tags, using the app's Settings option to delete all local data where available, or deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

Stackwich Forge does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user through the app, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
