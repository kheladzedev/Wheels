ShadeCluck Support

ShadeCluck is an offline-first yard route planning app for chicken keepers. It helps you compare shady, sheltered, dry walking routes, manage gates, run trail guard checklists, and keep daily flock walks organized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does ShadeCluck require an account?

No. ShadeCluck does not require an account or sign-in.

Does ShadeCluck work offline?

Yes. ShadeCluck is designed to work offline. Your yard catalog, routes, checklists, templates, gate timers, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Does ShadeCluck collect my personal data?

No. ShadeCluck does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal information.

Where is my yard and route data stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device. ShadeCluck does not use cloud sync or developer-operated servers.

How do gate timers and notifications work?

Gate timers and reminders use local iOS notifications when enabled. You can manage notification permissions in the app settings or through your iOS notification settings.

Can I share a checklist?

Yes. If the app offers sharing, it uses iOS system sharing features. You choose what to share and which app or service receives it.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual records inside the app, use the app's local data deletion option if available, or delete the app from your device.

Privacy

ShadeCluck is built with privacy in mind. The app does not require sign-in, does not use ads, does not track users, and does not collect personal information.

For more details, please read the ShadeCluck Privacy Policy.
