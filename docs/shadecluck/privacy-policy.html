ShadeCluck Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ShadeCluck handles information. ShadeCluck is an offline-first yard route planning app for chicken keepers, helping users manage zones, gates, shelters, routes, checklists, and local settings.

Summary

ShadeCluck does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal information. The app does not require an account, does not use cloud sync, does not include advertising, and does not track you.

Your yard items, routes, checklists, templates, gate timers, and settings stay on your device unless you choose to share something using iOS system features.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users of ShadeCluck.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, payment information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, device identifiers for tracking, or app activity for tracking.

Local App Data

ShadeCluck lets you create and store content locally on your device, including yard zones, gates, shelters, puddles, nests, hazard points, route order, today's plan, season and weather context, Trail Guard checklist items, reusable checklist templates, gate timer settings, local reminder preferences, theme, haptics, animations, and app settings.

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not receive, access, or upload this information.

Notifications and Gate Timers

ShadeCluck may offer optional local notifications for gate auto-close timers and checklist reminders. Notifications are handled locally through iOS. We do not collect notification-related personal information.

You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or in your device settings.

Sharing

ShadeCluck may let you share checklists or related app content using iOS system sharing features. Sharing is controlled by you and by the apps or services you choose to use. We do not receive or store anything you share through iOS system features.

Third-Party Services

ShadeCluck does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, or cloud storage services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information because we do not collect it.

Data Deletion

Because ShadeCluck stores app content locally on your device, you can delete your data by using the app's settings to delete local records, deleting individual yard items, routes, checklists, or templates inside the app, or deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

ShadeCluck does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
