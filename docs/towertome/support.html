Frequently Asked Questions

Does TowerTome require an account?

No. TowerTome does not require an account or sign-in.

Does TowerTome work offline?

Yes. TowerTome is designed to work offline. Your books, notes, quotes, tags, ratings, comparison history, goals, reminders, achievements, exports, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Does TowerTome collect personal reading content?

No. TowerTome does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal reading content through the app.

Where is my library stored?

Your library and reading tower data are stored locally on your device. TowerTome does not use cloud sync or developer-operated backend servers.

Can I export my library?

Yes. TowerTome can export a readable plain text summary through the native iOS share sheet. You choose what to share and which app or service receives it.

Why does TowerTome ask for Photos permission?

TowerTome may ask for Photos permission only if you choose to save a tower image to Photos. The app does not collect or upload your photo library.

How do reading reminders work?

Daily reading reminders use local iOS notifications when enabled. You can manage notification permissions in the app settings or through your iOS notification settings.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual books or comparison results, reset data from Settings where available, or delete the app from your device to remove local app data.

Privacy

TowerTome is built with privacy in mind. The app does not require sign-in, does not use ads, does not track users, and does not collect personal reading content through the app.

For more details, please read the TowerTome Privacy Policy.
