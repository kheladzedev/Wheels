TowerTome Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 5, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TowerTome handles information. TowerTome is an offline-first reading tracker that helps users organize books, ratings, notes, quotes, goals, comparisons, achievements, reminders, exports, and a colorful reading tower.

Summary

TowerTome does not collect, store, sell, rent, or share your personal reading content. The app does not require an account, does not use cloud sync, does not include advertising, and does not use analytics or tracking.

Your books, notes, quotes, tags, ratings, comparison history, goals, reminders, achievements, sample data changes, exports, and settings stay on your device unless you choose to share or export something using iOS system features.

Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information from users of TowerTome through the app.

The app does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location, contacts, payment information, usage analytics, advertising identifiers, device identifiers for tracking, app activity for tracking, or reading content.

Local App Data

TowerTome lets you create and store content locally on your device, including book titles, authors, status, ratings, dates, tags, purpose flags, notes, favorite quotes, manual order, filters, comparison choices, comparison history, ranked results, yearly goals, dashboard stats, achievements, reminder preferences, sample books, and app settings.

This information is stored locally on your device. We do not receive, access, or upload this information.

Notifications and Reading Reminders

TowerTome may offer optional daily reading reminders. Notifications are handled locally through iOS. We do not collect notification-related personal information.

You can disable notifications at any time in the app settings or in your device settings.

Photos Permission

TowerTome may ask for Photos permission if you choose to save a tower image to Photos. The permission is used only for the action you request. We do not access, collect, or upload your photo library.

Sharing and Export

TowerTome may let you export your library as plain text or share content using the native iOS share sheet. Sharing is controlled by you and by the apps or services you choose to use.

We do not receive or store anything you share through iOS system features.

Support Messages

If you contact support by email, we may receive the information you choose to send, such as your message, email address, device details, screenshots, or troubleshooting notes. We use support messages only to respond to your request, improve the app, and understand reported issues.

Third-Party Services

TowerTome does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking tools, backend servers, or cloud storage services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information. The app itself does not collect personal information or personal reading content.

Data Deletion

Because TowerTome stores app content locally on your device, you can delete your data by deleting individual books or comparison results, clearing or resetting data from Settings where available, or deleting the app from your device.

Children's Privacy

TowerTome does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user through the app, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the effective date above.
